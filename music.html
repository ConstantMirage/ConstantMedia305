Drake's Real Beef

6 Sues!!

In a shocking move, Drake's company, Frozen Moments, LLC, has filed two pre-action petitions against his parent record company Universal Music Group and Spotify.The first petition, filed in New York Supreme Court on Monday (Nov. 25), accuses the music conglomerate UMG of colluding with Spotify to artificially inflate Kendrick Lamar's "Not Like Us" diss song to Drizzy's detriment.

Lil Wayne's Redemption

Reunion

Long awaited reunion set lasted about 22 minutes, and featured the group’s turn-of-the-millennium hits like “Get Your Roll On,” “I Need a Hot Girl,” and “Neighborhood Superstar,” where the Hot Boys were joined by Big Tymers’ Mannie Fresh and Birdman. Each member also delved into their own catalogs, with Juvenile performing his “Back That Azz Up” and B.G. leading his “Bling Bling.”.

Tyler The Creator

Chromakopia

Chromakopia topping the charts is a testament to Tyler’s journey from his early days in the hip-hop collective Odd Future to becoming a solo artist with a massive fanbase. Over the years, Tyler has explored various sounds and styles, blending genres in unexpected ways. This number one debut reflects his ability to captivate listeners while staying true to his distinct artistic vision.

Kendrick Poppin Out

GNX

GNX builds upon Kendrick's critically acclaimed 2022 album, Mr. Morale and the Big Steppers, which marked his triumphant return after a five-year hiatus. While Mr. Morale reflected a deeply introspective narrative, GNX leans into storytelling that’s both expansive and incendiary, challenging societal norms and personal adversaries.
