Lil Weezyana Fest

In Full Effect

Lil Wayne’s Lil Weezyana Fest is returning to New Orleans for another massive event. On Wednesday (Sept. 3), the “Lollipop” rapper announced the 2024 lineup, leaving fans thrilled to learn that he will reunite with Hot Boys — comprised of himself, Juvenile, B.G. and Turk — at the Smoothie King Center on Nov. 2.

Lil Wayne embodies the spirit of New Orleans, and Lil Weezyana Fest is a stellar showcase of the city’s dynamic and flourishing music scene,”.“Live Nation Urban celebrates this amazing event.

"During a sit-down with YG for the “4HUNNID Podcast,” Lil Wayne teased that a reunion was certainly in the cards, even in light of its members beefing.

The group’s homecoming was largely delayed due to B.G.’s 14-year prison sentence for gun possession and witness tampering. He was released to a warm welcome from the Hip Hop community in 2023.

"The “A Milli” rapper appeared overcome with gratitude as the honors were bestowed upon him. But while addressing the crowd, he circled back to the Super Bowl controversy that saw Kendrick Lamar selected to headline the Apple Music halftime show in September.
